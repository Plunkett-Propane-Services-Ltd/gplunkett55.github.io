ABOUT US

Meet The Team

Founded in 2005, Plunkett Propane Services Ltd has been a trusted name in heating and propane appliance solutions throughout Bancroft and the surrounding area. We specialize in furnaces, fireplaces, ductwork, propane water heaters, air conditioners, standby generators, and everything in between with a strong focus on new construction homes and cottages. Additionally, we specialize in converting wood and oil heating systems to propane, guiding you through the process from start to finish for a seamless transition. Our experienced team is dedicated to delivering top-quality installations and customized solutions to ensure your comfort year-round. With three generations of experience, you can trust Plunkett Propane Services Ltd for reliable service, expert advice, and exceptional craftsmanship.
